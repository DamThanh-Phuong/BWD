Thanh Hoa

Thanh Hoa, located in the northern central region of Vietnam, is a hidden gem for travelers. Known for its stunning landscapes and rich history, it offers a variety of attractions. Sam Son Beach, with its golden sands and clear waters, is perfect for a relaxing getaway. The ancient citadel of Ho Dynasty, a UNESCO World Heritage site, provides a glimpse into Vietnam's imperial past. Additionally, the Ben En National Park is a must-visit for nature enthusiasts, boasting diverse flora and fauna. Whether you're interested in history, nature, or simply unwinding by the sea, Thanh Hoa has something to offer every visitor.

Nghe An

Nghe An, a province in north-central Vietnam, is famous for its beautiful landscapes and cultural heritage. Visitors can explore the stunning beaches of Cua Lo, visit the historical Kim Lien village, the birthplace of President Ho Chi Minh, and enjoy the natural beauty of Pu Mat National Park. The region is also known for its rich traditions, folk songs, and festivals. Nghe An offers a unique blend of history, culture, and natural beauty for travelers to discover.
